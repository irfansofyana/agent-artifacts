Budget sanity

Healthy venue-side target is around IDR 30-45 juta. IDR 50-60 juta can work only when the venue clearly earns it. Above IDR 70 juta starts to pressure a 200 juta total budget.

How to read Tiga Dara

The Tiga Dara column is a venue+catering benchmark for 500 guests, not venue-only. It is useful for comparing total direction, but inclusions still need checking.
